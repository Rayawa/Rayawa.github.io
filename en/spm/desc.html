New Features

Sweet Potato
A kind of food added is called sweet potato. It has three categories purple, red and white. Sweet potato powder and peel is also added.              
Magic Cube
Magic Cube has a black texture. In an activated Magic Cube, you can transfer one category of Raw Sweet Potato to another, or enchant it to Enchanted Sweet Potato.              
Grinder
Grinder's function is to grind Sweet Potatoes into Sweet Potato Powder.               
Agroforestry Table
The working place of Agroforestry Table is made of gold. It can enchant sweet potatoes, saplings and crops.                
Enchanted Saplings and Crops
Enchanted saplings and crops grew more faster than vanilla ones, the also grew up as enchanted ones.
