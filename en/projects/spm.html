Sweet Potato Mod

Where agriculture meets magic! A Minecraft mod co-developed with @teddyxlandlee, adding sweet potatoes, agricultural stations, grinders, magic pots, enchanted crops, and more.

Pigeonia Featurehouse GitHub Download

New Content

Sweet Potato

A food category added by this mod. It comes in purple, red, and white varieties, each with three sub-variants, plus sweet potato powder and peels.

Magic Cube

The magic cube emanates a dark glow. Activate it by lighting soul fire in the 3×3 area below, converting three raw sweet potatoes of any variety into other varieties or randomly enchanting them.

Grinder

The grinder has a millstone that grinds sweet potatoes into sweet potato powder.

Enchanted Saplings & Crops

Enchanted saplings and crops grow faster than vanilla ones and will grow into enchanted trees or crops.

Notices

  • This mod currently only supports Fabric 1.16+. Earlier versions are not supported.
  • Do not repost this mod to other platforms without the authors' permission!
  • Loading this mod requires Fabric Loader and Fabric API. The Loader version must be at least 0.9.0; the API should be the latest version.
  • Do not add content from this mod to your modpack without the authors' permission!
  • To use this mod on a server, install it on both the client and server sides.
  • Report bugs or suggestions on GitHub Issues.
